# Animatons by parameter.
### Wings
- `0`: no wings
- `1`: unspread
- `2`: spread wing
- `3`: flapping wing

###CVR_Flight
- `Wings>0` & `T`: flapping wing
- `Wings==0` & `T`: No wings
- `F`: [Wings Determined]

###Avatar_Flight
- `Wings>0` & `T`: flapping wing
- `Wings==0` & `T`: No wings
- `F`: [Wings Determined]
